About The Primary

Brynno International School, is a school aimed at nurturing and training youths in intellectual excellence with sound moral values as well as sound academic knowledge. We believe that if a child is raised in the way he/she should go, (the way of the Lord), he will not depart from it he matures. The Word of God says it and we believe it wholeheartedly and are earnestly committed to achieving it.
