About The Proprietress - Dr.(Mrs) BRIDGET MGBEUDO CHIGBO.

I

introduce to you a humble, beautiful, intelligent, honest, God fearing, amiable and hardworking woman, Dr. (Mrs.) Bridget M. Chigbo. Who has distinguished herself in the education sector.

Born on 7th October, 1970 in Aba, Abia State, she attended Old Count Primary School, Aba, Ohabiam secondary school Aba and Afugiri Girls Secondary School Umuahia, where she finished her Secondary Education and graduated in 1988 as the Adanna House Prefect. Thereafter, she was admitted into the prestigious Alvan Ikoku College of Education, Now University of Education, Owerri, where she studied French/English, finished in 1993. Immediately after that, she got admission to study English (B.Ed) at University of Nigeria Nsukka in 1994.

The quest for further self and capacity development led her to obtain (M.Ed) in Educational Administration from Ignatius Ajuru University of Education in 2019. She hit the top most academic ladder by clinching (Ph.D) in Educational Administration, Ignatius Ajuru university of Education in 2023.

Dr.(Mrs) Bridget M. Chigbo is a Professional and enthusiastic teacher she has a teaching experience for over 20years, currently serving as a deputy Principal and school Administrator for over 15 years. She is the Proprietress of Brynno International School, Port Harcourt, River State Nigeria. She is married to one husband and has children.

Dr.(Mrs) Bridget Chigbo belongs to several Professional bodies; these includes (NAPPS) National Association of Proprietors of Private Schools, Association for Progressive and Alternative Education in Nigeria (ASPROADIN), Institution leadership and capacity building in Africa (ILCBA). She has also written published articles in both local and international journals.
